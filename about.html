Career

Technician

Engineer in training

I continued my studies in an engineering school on a work-study basis. Every two months, I alternated between school 3il Rodez (studying network, programming, mathematics, etc.) and, again, Edokial (working on client/server projects, object-oriented applications, etc.).

The last year of school was special, because thanks to good grades, I went to Québec in UQAC for 4 months and came back 8 months in Edokial for a big project:

In order to facilitate the decision-making process of the director of printing and enveloping at Edokial, I created with him a Business Intelligence tool retrieving all the data of his production in order to make a personalized summary in real time. He was very glad of my work and I got easily graduated also thanks to him.

Engineer

After graduating as an engineer, I continued to work at Edokial thanks to a partnership with ISAE Supaéro.

I worked with a lecturer on blockchain, and more particularly on cryptographic protocols (such as proof of storage), in order to:

  • Firstly, train myself on blockchain for Edokial
  • And secondly, on the other hand, to carry out research work for ISAE.
Once trained, I was able to lead blockchain-related projects independently at Edokial. This resulted in delivering three proofs of concept on various topics, including one involving a customer.
